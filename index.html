Driving Innovation and Growth in the African iGaming Industry

We are a leading organization dedicated to promoting responsible gaming practices and fostering innovation within the African gambling sector.

Our mission is to ensure that the industry grows sustainably while prioritizing consumer protection and ethical standards.

We don’t just talk about values- we live them. From the way we collaborate to the impact we strive to make, our actions reflect a dedication to fairness, transparency, and meaningful progress.

  • Advocating for responsible gaming initiatives across Africa.
  • Promoting fair play and transparency in online gambling platforms.
  • Supporting local businesses and entrepreneurs in the igaming industry.

Join our community today and help shape the future of the African iGaming industry!
